### SAFFRAT RMS DATABASE SCHEMA GUIDE (ENRICHED)

**LOGIC & ENUM NOTES:**
- **GLAccounts.Category**: 0=Asset, 1=Liability, 2=Equity, 3=Revenue, 4=Expense
- **GLAccounts.Type**: 0=CashAndBank, 1=Receivable, 2=Inventory, 4=Payable, 10=Sales, 13=FoodCost, 15=Labor.
- **Employees.Status**: 1=Active, 0=Inactive.
- **Orders.Status**: 0=Pending, 1=Completed, 2=Cancelled.
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Most tables have `TenantId`. Filter by current tenant context if applicable.
