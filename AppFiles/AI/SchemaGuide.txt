### SAFFRAT RMS DATABASE SCHEMA GUIDE

This guide explains the relationships and data available in the system for dynamic report generation.

#### 1. Sales & Orders
- **Orders**: `Id`, `Total`, `SubTotal`, `DiscountTotal`, `TaxTotal`, `ChargeTotal`, `PaidAmount`, `DueAmount`, `CustomerId`, `CreatedAt`.
- **OrderDetails**: `OrderId`, `FoodItemId` (linked to `Item`), `Quantity`, `Price`, `Total`.
- **PaymentMethods**: `MethodName`.

#### 2. Products & Inventory
- **FoodItems**: `ItemName`, `Price`, `FoodGroupId`.
- **FoodGroups**: `Name`.
- **IngredientItems**: `ItemName`, `Quantity`, `AlertQuantity`, `Unit`.
- **Purchases**: `SupplierId`, `TotalAmount`, `PurchaseDate`.

#### 3. Customers & Suppliers
- **Customers**: `CustomerName`, `Email`, `Phone`.
- **Suppliers**: `SupplierName`, `Email`, `Phone`.

#### 4. HRM & Staff
- **Employees**: `FullName`, `Email`, `Phone`, `DepartmentId`, `DesignationId`, `Salary`.
- **Attendances**: `EmployeeId`, `AttendaceDate`, `ClockIn`, `ClockOut`, `Status`.
- **Departments**: `Name`.
- **Designations**: `Name`.

#### 5. Accounting
- **GLAccounts**: `AccountName`, `AccountCode`.
- **JournalEntries**: `Debit`, `Credit`, `Narration`, `CreatedAt`.
- **Invoices**: `InvoiceNumber`, `TotalAmount`, `DueDate`.
- **Bills**: `BillNumber`, `TotalAmount`, `DueDate`.

#### 6. Infrastructure
- **RestaurantTables**: `TableName`, `Capacity`.
- **WorkPeriods**: `StartDate`, `EndDate`, `IsOpened`.
