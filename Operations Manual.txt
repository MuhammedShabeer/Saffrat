### 1. Introduction <a name="introduction"></a>
The **Al Markiya Business Management Suite** is a centralized ERP for enterprise-level operations. This manual focuses on data integrity, specifically in the accounting and inventory modules.

---

### 2. Work Period & Cash Reconciliation <a name="work-period"></a>
Every business day **MUST** begin and end with a Work Period.
- **Opening a Session**: Navigate to **Sale -> Cash Register**. Enter the "Opening Balance" (the cash currently in the drawer). This starts the tracking period.
- **Closing a Session**: At the end of the shift, return to **Cash Register** and select **End Session**. Enter the "Closing Balance". 
- **Verification**: The system will automatically calculate the difference between expected sales and actual cash, generating a reconciliation report.

---

### 3. Point of Sale (POS) Operations <a name="pos-operations"></a>
- **Active Orders**: Use the **POS** screen to select tables. Items added here update the **Kitchen Display** in real-time.
- **Finalizing Sales**: Once a customer pays, click **Settle**. Choose the payment method (Cash/Card). Closing the order triggers an automatic Journal Entry:
    - *Debit: Cash/Bank Account*
    - *Credit: Sales Revenue*

---

### 4. Inventory & Purchasing <a name="inventory-management"></a>
**A. Adding Stock (Purchases)**
1. Navigate to **Purchase -> Add Purchase**.
2. Select the **Supplier** and enter their **Invoice Number**.
3. Add items and specify the **Purchase Price** and **Quantity**.
4. **Saving**: Upon save, the system updates ingredient quantities and creates an Accounts Payable (Bill) or reduces Cash if paid immediately.

**B. Stock Adjustments & Wastage**
1. Navigate to **Ingredients -> Stock Adjustment**.
2. Select **New Adjustment**.
3. Choose the item and the type (**Wastage** or **Manual Correction**).
4. Enter the quantity and a mandatory reason (e.g., "Expired"). This ensures the P&L reflects the loss.

### 5. Accounting Data Entry Guide <a name="accounting-guide"></a>
The system uses automated background accounting to ensure accuracy.

**A. Chart of Accounts (COA)**
- Found under **Accounting -> Chart of Accounts**.
- Categories: **0=Asset, 1=Liability, 2=Equity, 3=Revenue, 4=Expense**.
- Each account tracks its `CurrentBalance` automatically.

**B. Entering Manual Transactions (Cash Ledger)**
For day-to-day office expenses or small income:
1. Go to **Accounting -> Cash Ledger**.
2. Click **Add Transaction**.
3. Select **Expense** (e.g., Office Supplies) or **Income**.
4. Choose the specific **Offset Account** (e.g., Electricity Expense).
5. Enter the amount and description.
- *Tip: This replaces manual petty cash books and syncs directly with your Balance Sheet.*

**C. Managing Invoices & Bills**
- **AR Invoices**: For corporate clients who pay later.
- **AP Bills**: For supplier credit.
- Navigate to **Accounting -> AR Invoices** or **AP Bills** to track payment status (Paid/Unpaid/Partial).

---

### 6. Partner Equity & Dashboard <a name="partner-equity"></a>
Designed for transparent capital management.
1. **Adding Partners**: Go to **Accounting -> Partner Equity**. Add a partner to generate their unique Equity Account.
2. **Managing Capital**:
    - **Investments**: Use this when a partner injects cash.
    - **Withdrawals**: For partner drawings.
    - **Profit Distribution**: Distribute business earnings.
3. **Visuals**: Use the Dashboard to see real-time ownership percentages and current net equity balances.

---

### 7. HRM & Payroll <a name="hrm"></a>
- **Attendance**: Staff must clock in/out via the dashboard.
- **Payroll Generation**: At the end of the month, use **HRM -> Payroll** to generate slips. The system calculates Net Salary based on base pay, deductions, and bonuses.

---

### 8. AI-Powered Reporting <a name="reporting"></a>
Don't wait for monthly reports.
1. Go to **Reports -> AI Assistant**.
2. Ask questions like:
    - *"Show me the sales trend for last week."*
    - *"Who is our most active supplier?"*
    - *"What is our current inventory value?"*
3. The AI analyzes real-time database schema to provide instant, professional insights.

---

> [!WARNING]
> Manual Journal Entries should only be performed by authorized accountants. Most business transactions (Sales, Purchases, Payroll) are handled automatically by the system.
